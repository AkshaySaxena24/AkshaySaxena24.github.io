WHO ARE WE?

Welcome to Cultura, where innovation meets agriculture. Meet Kruti, our cutting-edge agri bot that revolutionizes farming practices. Kruti seamlessly connects to your phone via Bluetooth, offering a user-friendly interface for precision farming. With automated movement, Kruti efficiently navigates through fields, ensuring optimal coverage for tasks such as pesticide and water spraying. Its advanced technology extends to seed dispensing, making it a versatile solution for various crops, including paddy cultivation.
